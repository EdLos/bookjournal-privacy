Privacy Policy

My Life is all BOOKed

Effective Date: April 10, 2026

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

Summary: My Life is all BOOKed does not collect, transmit, or share any of your personal data. Everything stays on your device.

1. Introduction

Welcome to My Life is all BOOKed ("the App"), a personal reading journal application for Android developed by the creator ("we," "our," or "us").

This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in connection with your use of My Life is all BOOKed. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Please read this policy carefully.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

My Life is all BOOKed is designed with privacy as a core principle. We do not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Reading habits, book lists, or review content
  • Payment information
  • Any other personal data

3. How Your Data Is Stored

All data you enter into My Life is all BOOKed — including your book reviews, reading lists, favourites, reading statistics, and app settings — is stored exclusively and locally on your device using your device's local storage (browser localStorage via a WebView).

This means:

  • Your data never leaves your device
  • We have no access to your data
  • Your data is not transmitted to any server, cloud service, or third party
  • Your data is not backed up to external servers (only to your device's own backup system, if enabled by you in your device settings)

4. Third-Party Services

My Life is all BOOKed does not integrate any third-party services that collect data, including:

  • No analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, Mixpanel, etc.)
  • No advertising networks
  • No crash reporting services
  • No social media SDKs

Capacitor Framework: The App is built using the Capacitor framework, which provides a WebView container for the web application. Capacitor itself does not collect user data.

4a. Advertising (Google AdMob)

My Life is all BOOKed displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and use certain data to serve ads, including:

  • Android Advertising ID
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Approximate location (country/region level)
  • App interaction data

This data is collected by Google, not by us. You can opt out of personalized advertising in your Android device settings under Privacy → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

Users can permanently remove all ads via the in-app "Remove Ads" purchase.

4b. In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time in-app purchase ("Remove Ads – €2.99") processed by Google Play Billing. We do not collect or store any payment information. All payment processing is handled by Google. See Google Payments Privacy Notice.

5. Permissions

My Life is all BOOKed may request the following Android permissions:

Permission Purpose Data Sent Externally
READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE / READ_MEDIA_IMAGES To allow you to select book cover images from your gallery No — images are stored locally
WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE To save exported files (CSV export feature) No — files are saved to your device
INTERNET Required by WebView; no network requests made by the App No active connections made

We do not request permissions beyond what is necessary for the App's functionality.

6. Goodreads CSV Import/Export

The App includes a feature to import and export reading data in CSV format (compatible with Goodreads exports).

  • Import: CSV files are read from your device's local storage and processed locally. No data is transmitted to any server.
  • Export: CSV files are generated from your local data and saved to your device. No data is transmitted externally.

7. Children's Privacy

My Life is all BOOKed does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Since the App collects no personal information whatsoever, it is safe for users of all ages.

8. Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, the security of your data is protected by your device's own security mechanisms (screen lock, device encryption, etc.). We encourage you to use a strong screen lock on your device.

9. Data Deletion

To delete all data stored by My Life is all BOOKed:

  1. Clear App Data: Go to your Android Settings → Apps → My Life is all BOOKed → Storage → Clear Data
  2. Uninstall the App: Uninstalling My Life is all BOOKed will remove all locally stored data.

We have no ability to delete data on your behalf, as we do not have access to it.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

Continued use of the App after any changes to this Privacy Policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at the email address provided in the app or on the app store listing.

This privacy policy was last updated on April 10, 2026.

Alle Daten, die du in My Life is all BOOKed eingibst — einschließlich deiner Buchrezensionen, Leselisten, Favoriten, Lesestatistiken und App-Einstellungen — werden ausschließlich und lokal auf deinem Gerät gespeichert.

My Life is all BOOKed integriert keine Drittanbieter-Dienste, die Daten sammeln, einschließlich:

  • Keine Social-Media-SDKs

My Life is all BOOKed zeigt Werbung von Google AdMob. AdMob kann bestimmte Daten erheben, darunter:

Mehr Informationen: Google Datenschutzrichtlinie.

Die App bietet einen einmaligen In-App-Kauf ("Werbung entfernen – €2,99"), abgewickelt über Google Play Billing. Wir erfassen oder speichern keine Zahlungsdaten. Alle Zahlungen werden von Google verarbeitet.

WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE Um exportierte Dateien zu speichern (CSV-Export-Funktion) Nein — Dateien werden auf deinem Gerät gespeichert

Die App enthält eine Funktion zum Importieren und Exportieren von Lesedaten im CSV-Format (kompatibel mit Goodreads-Exporten).

  • Import: CSV-Dateien werden aus dem lokalen Speicher deines Geräts gelesen und lokal verarbeitet. Keine Daten werden an einen Server übertragen.
  • Export: CSV-Dateien werden aus deinen lokalen Daten generiert und auf deinem Gerät gespeichert. Keine Daten werden extern übertragen.

My Life is all BOOKed erhebt wissentlich keine Informationen von Kindern unter 13 Jahren. Da die App keine persönlichen Informationen sammelt, ist sie für Benutzer jeden Alters sicher.

9. Datenlöschung

Um alle von My Life is all BOOKed gespeicherten Daten zu löschen:

  1. App-Daten löschen: Gehe zu deinen Android-Einstellungen → Apps → My Life is all BOOKed → Speicher → Daten löschen

Wir können Daten nicht in deinem Namen löschen, da wir keinen Zugriff darauf haben.
